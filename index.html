Apollo 11

First Launched in 1969!

Apollo 11, launched on July 16, 1969, was the first spacecraft to land humans on the moon. Astronauts Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin made history by stepping onto the lunar surface during this mission.

The Milkyway

Not really made out of milk

The Milky Way is a vast spiral galaxy with billions of stars, gas, dust, and other celestial objects. It spans about 100,000 light-years and is part of the Local Group of galaxies. Its mesmerizing spiral arms and radiant band of light make it a captivating sight in the night sky, revealing the beauty and grandeur of our cosmic neighborhood.

Our History

SpaceShop is a renowned gift shop located at Kennedy Space Center, founded in 1999.

We offers visitors captivating tours that allow them to explore the wonders of space firsthand. Alongside these unforgettable journeys, SpaceShop boasts an extraordinary gift shop filled with celestial treasures. From astronaut memorabilia to space-themed souvenirs, SpaceShop provides an exceptional shopping experience for space enthusiasts of all ages. With its rich history and remarkable offerings, SpaceShop continues to be a destination that combines education, exploration, and an out-of-this-world shopping experience.

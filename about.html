About Us

At SpaceShop, our passion for space exploration led us to establish a remarkable gift shop at Kennedy Space Center in 1999. As proud owners, we have dedicated ourselves to creating an exceptional experience for visitors who share our fascination with the wonders of the universe.

When you visit SpaceShop, you'll not only embark on captivating tours that transport you into the depths of space but also discover an extraordinary gift shop that reflects our love for all things celestial. Our meticulously curated collection showcases a wide range of astronaut memorabilia, awe-inspiring space-themed souvenirs, and unique treasures that capture the essence of space exploration.

Step inside our doors and immerse yourself in a world where imagination and scientific curiosity intertwine. From stunning models of spacecraft to authentic meteorite fragments, each item in our gift shop tells a story and offers a tangible connection to the vastness of the cosmos.

Our knowledgeable staff members are passionate about space and are always ready to share their expertise, guiding you through our extensive selection and helping you find the perfect memento to commemorate your visit. Whether you're searching for a symbolic keepsake, an educational toy for a young aspiring astronaut, or a special gift for a space enthusiast in your life, SpaceShop has something extraordinary to offer.

We take great pride in being part of the Kennedy Space Center experience, and our mission is to provide visitors with an unparalleled blend of education, inspiration, and retail therapy. We invite you to join us on this celestial journey, where every purchase supports our shared love for space exploration and the continued advancement of human understanding of the cosmos.

So, come explore SpaceShop, where tours and an amazing gift shop converge to create an out-of-this-world experience that will leave you with lasting memories and a deeper appreciation for the marvels of our universe.
